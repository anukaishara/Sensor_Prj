Arduino Uno:

Digital Pins:

PIR Motion Sensor: Connect output to Digital Pin 2

RFID Module (MFRC522): Standard SPI pins:

SDA to Digital Pin 10
SCK to Digital Pin 13
MOSI to Digital Pin 11
MISO to Digital Pin 12
RST to Digital Pin 9

Vibration Sensor: Connect output to Digital Pin 3

Servo Motor: Connect control pin to Digital Pin 6

Buzzer: Connect positive to Digital Pin 7

Red LED: Connect to Digital Pin 4

Green LED: Connect to Digital Pin 5

I2C Pins (for LCD):
SDA to A4
SCL to A5
